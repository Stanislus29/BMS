* KiCad ngspice Fully-Compatible 5-Pin LDO (0.8V VREF)
* Node Order: IN OUT GND EN FB

.SUBCKT TLV767 IN OUT GND EN FB

* --- 1. Enable Pin Control Logic ---
R_EN     EN  GND 100k
S_EN     INT_VREF GND EN GND SW_EN 
.MODEL   SW_EN SW(vt=1.0 vh=0.2 ron=0.01 roff=1Meg)
R_PULL   INT_VREF GND 1Meg

* --- 2. Internal Reference (0.8V) ---
V_REF    INT_REF_SRC GND DC 0.8

* --- 3. Robust Error Amplifier (Native ngspice VCVS) ---
* This acts as an op-amp comparing INT_VREF (+) to FB (-).
* Gain is set to 1000. Output goes to the internal GATE node.
E_AMP    IN GATE INT_VREF FB 1000

* --- 4. PMOS Pass Transistor ---
* Driven directly by the GATE node relative to IN.
M_PASS   OUT GATE IN IN PMOS_LDO
.MODEL   PMOS_LDO PMOS (VTO=-1.0 KP=20 CGS=10p CGD=10p)

* --- 5. Quiescent Current & Stabilizing Capacitance ---
R_IQ     IN GND 50k
C_OUT    OUT GND 1p

.ENDS TLV767
